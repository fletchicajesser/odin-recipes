Eleni's Peach Pasta

Description

This recipe was sent to me by my dear friend Eleni. She's always been the most creative person I know (and an amazing cook!) and was inspired by the delectable peaches in southern California to create this beautiful dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 peaches, chopped (you can keep the skin on!)
  • Cherry tomatoes, cut in half
  • 2 sliced garlic cloves
  • Chopped basil
  • Calabrian chilli paste

    • Steps

    1. Put some oil in a pan and saute the garlic over medium heat.
    2. Add the peaches and tomatoes once the garlic is soft.
    3. Saute a bit until soft, then add basil.
    4. Cook down until juices release, then add calabrian chilli paste
    5. Add a little pasta water to the sauce, then blend.
    6. Drain pasta, add a little butter, then mix with the sauce.
    7. Garnish with parmesean and basil.
